About Adventure Isles

About Adventure Isles

Adventure Isles: Farm, Explore is a family-friendly farming and adventure simulation game set on a mysterious island.
Join Arya as she wakes up on an island full of unexplained phenomena, and begin your journey of exploration and survival.

  • Farm and Harvest:

    Grow crops, raise animals, and manage your farm to sustain the island's inhabitants.

  • Uncover Mysteries:

    Explore the island’s secrets, complete quests, and solve ancient puzzles.

  • Workshops and Upgrades:

    Build and upgrade workshops to process your crops and meet the needs of the islanders.

  • Explore with Friends:

    Adventure with your companions, discovering treasures and hidden corners of the island.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start farming in Adventure Isles?

To start farming, you’ll need to clear some land on the island and then begin planting crops. You can also take care of animals by feeding them and collecting their produce. Your crops and animals will provide the resources needed to upgrade your buildings.

What are the mysteries on the island?

The island is full of secrets, including mysterious symbols on huge stone slabs, flickering figures, and glowing portals. Completing quests and exploring different areas of the island will reveal more about these secrets and unlock hidden treasures.

Can I play the game offline?

Yes, you can enjoy Adventure Isles offline. Once the game is downloaded, you can continue your farming, exploration, and quests without an internet connection.

What is the workshop system?

Workshops are essential buildings on your farm that allow you to process the products you harvest. For example, you can build a mill to grind wheat or a forge to make tools. Upgrading these workshops will help you keep up with the islanders’ growing needs.

How do I unlock new areas of the island?

As you progress in the game, you will unlock new maps and areas to explore. This is typically done by completing certain quests or discovering new locations through the exploration system.

What kinds of quests can I complete?

Quests in Adventure Isles range from simple tasks, such as gathering resources, to more complex missions involving uncovering the island's mysteries and solving puzzles. Completing quests will reward you with coins, items, and story progression.

How can I level up my hero, Arya?

Arya levels up as you complete quests and uncover secrets. You can also improve her skills by gathering resources and completing specific tasks related to her adventure.

Is the game suitable for all ages?

Yes, Adventure Isles: Farm, Explore is designed to be family-friendly, with relaxing farming mechanics and an exciting adventure narrative suitable for players of all ages.

What types of animals can I have on my farm?

There are several types of animals you can raise on your farm, such as chickens, cows, and goats. Each animal provides different resources like eggs, milk, and wool, which are essential for crafting and upgrading buildings.

Can I play with friends?

Currently, Adventure Isles is a single-player experience, but you can share your progress with friends and compare your achievements and farm upgrades.

Are there in-app purchases in the game?

Yes, there are in-app purchases for items that can help speed up progress or unlock special features. However, all content in the game can be unlocked through regular gameplay.
